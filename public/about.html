About Animation & Black History

Animation has played a significant role in shaping Black culture, from representation in anime to inspiring Black creators worldwide.

Many Black artists and writers have taken inspiration from anime, incorporating its storytelling and visual techniques into their own work.

Known for: Nahoya "Smiley" Kawata (Tokyo Revengers), Typhon (Re:Zero)

Known for: Takemichi Hanagaki (Tokyo Revengers), Jyugo (Nanbaka)
